# Trust Report

> Computed view. Refreshed as a side-effect of `wiki-lint`. Do not hand-edit —
> changes will be overwritten on the next lint run.
>
> Composes the four reliability signals into a per-page trust score:
> contradictions, supersessions, open gaps, volatility/staleness.

## Scoring

- **high** — no signals.
- **medium** — 1–2 signals.
- **low** — 3+ signals, or any unresolved `[!contradiction]`.
