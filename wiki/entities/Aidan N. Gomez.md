# Aidan N. Gomez

Aidan N. Gomez is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. He was affiliated with [[University of Toronto]] while the Transformer work was performed at [[Google Brain]]. With [[Łukasz Kaiser]] he spent long stretches designing parts of [[tensor2tensor]] and replacing the team's earlier codebase, greatly accelerating the research (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed and implemented significant parts of [[tensor2tensor]].
- Helped replace the team's earlier codebase with tensor2tensor, improving results and throughput.

## Connections

- Co-author with: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Illia Polosukhin]].
