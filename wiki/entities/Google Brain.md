# Google Brain

Google Brain is a deep-learning research team at Google. At the time of [[Attention Is All You Need]] (2017), four of the paper's eight authors — [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], and [[Aidan N. Gomez]] (visiting from [[University of Toronto]]) — listed Google Brain as their affiliation. Google Brain also contributed the [[tensor2tensor]] framework used to train the models.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the [[Transformer]] research program.
- Produced [[tensor2tensor]].

## Connections

- Related org: [[Google Research]] (both are parts of Google's research organization; merged in 2023 as Google DeepMind but distinct at the time of the paper).
- Members: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]].
