# Jakob Uszkoreit

Jakob Uszkoreit is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. Per the author contributions footnote, he **proposed replacing RNNs with self-attention** and started the effort to evaluate the idea — the initial spark for the [[Transformer]] (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).

## Key Contributions

- Proposed replacing recurrent layers with [[Self-Attention]] in sequence transduction.
- Started and organized the research effort that produced the [[Transformer]].
- Earlier co-authored "A Decomposable Attention Model" (Parikh, Täckström, Das, Uszkoreit, EMNLP 2016) — cited as [22] and an early example of attention-only models.

## Connections

- Affiliated with: [[Google Research]].
- Co-author with: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Illia Polosukhin]].
- Developed: [[Transformer]], [[Self-Attention]]-based NMT.
