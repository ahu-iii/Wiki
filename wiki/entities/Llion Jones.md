# Llion Jones

Llion Jones is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. At [[Google Research]] he owned the team's initial codebase, contributed novel model variants, and drove efficient inference and attention visualizations (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).

## Key Contributions

- Built and maintained the team's initial codebase that preceded [[tensor2tensor]].
- Experimented with novel model variants of the [[Transformer]].
- Work on efficient inference and visualizing attention distributions (Appendix figures).
