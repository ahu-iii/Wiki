# Niki Parmar

Niki Parmar is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. At [[Google Research]] she designed, implemented, tuned, and evaluated countless model variants in the team's original codebase and in [[tensor2tensor]] (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).

## Key Contributions

- Designed, implemented, tuned, and evaluated many [[Transformer]] variants.
- Contributed to the [[tensor2tensor]] codebase used to produce the paper's results.

## Connections

- Affiliated with: [[Google Research]].
- Co-author with: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Illia Polosukhin]].
- Developed: [[Transformer]].
