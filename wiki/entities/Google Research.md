# Google Research

Google Research is Google's broad research division. In the author list of [[Attention Is All You Need]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], and [[Illia Polosukhin]] listed Google Research as their affiliation.
