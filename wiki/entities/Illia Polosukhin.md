# Illia Polosukhin

Illia Polosukhin is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. Work on the paper was performed at [[Google Research]]. With [[Ashish Vaswani]] he designed and implemented the first [[Transformer]] models (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed and implemented the first [[Transformer]] models.

## Connections

- Work performed at: [[Google Research]].
- Co-author with: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]].
- Developed: [[Transformer]].
