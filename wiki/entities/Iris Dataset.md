# Iris Dataset

> The Iris dataset is one of the classical small tabular datasets bundled with [[scikit-learn]] ([[Introduction to Machine Learning with Python (Muller & Guido)]]). In the Chapter 3 excerpt it is referenced as a recommended real-world dataset for the reader's own clustering and decomposition experiments, alongside the [[Digits Dataset]] and [[Breast Cancer Dataset]] ([[Introduction to Machine Learning with Python (Muller & Guido)]]).

## Key Facts

- Mentioned explicitly at the end of the chapter as one of the "real-world datasets included in scikit-learn" recommended for further practice with clustering and decomposition methods ([[Introduction to Machine Learning with Python (Muller & Guido)]]).

> [!gap]
> Chapter 3 of this source does not describe Iris's sample count, features, or species labels in detail; that information should come from future ingests or earlier chapters of the book. Commonly-cited 150-sample / 4-feature / 3-class figures are **not** established from this excerpt. (?)
