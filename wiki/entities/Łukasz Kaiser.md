# Łukasz Kaiser

Łukasz Kaiser is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. At [[Google Brain]] he — together with [[Aidan N. Gomez]] — designed and implemented major parts of [[tensor2tensor]] and replaced the team's earlier codebase, significantly improving results. Also co-authored the Neural GPU and "Can Active Memory Replace Attention" papers cited by the Transformer.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed and implemented large parts of [[tensor2tensor]] (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).
- Co-authored "Neural GPUs learn algorithms" (Kaiser & Sutskever, ICLR 2016; cited as [14]).
- Co-authored "Can active memory replace attention?" (Kaiser & Bengio, NIPS 2016; cited as [20]).
