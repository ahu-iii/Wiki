# Ashish Vaswani

Ashish Vaswani is the first (equal-contribution) author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. At the time of publication he was at [[Google Brain]]. Per the author contributions footnote, he — with [[Illia Polosukhin]] — designed and implemented the first [[Transformer]] models and was crucially involved in every aspect of the work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed and implemented the first [[Transformer]] models (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).
- Crucial involvement across model design, implementation, and evaluation.

## Connections

- Affiliated with: [[Google Brain]].
- Co-author with: [[Noam Shazeer]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Illia Polosukhin]].
- Developed: [[Transformer]].
