# University of Toronto

The University of Toronto is a Canadian public research university with a strong machine-learning research lineage (birthplace of AlexNet and much of modern deep learning). In [[Attention Is All You Need]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]] is listed with a University of Toronto affiliation; the footnote notes his work on the paper was performed at [[Google Brain]].
