# Noam Shazeer

Noam Shazeer is an equal-contribution co-author of [[Attention Is All You Need]]. At [[Google Brain]] he proposed [[Scaled Dot-Product Attention]], [[Multi-Head Attention]], and the parameter-free position representation, and was involved in nearly every detail of the work.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed [[Scaled Dot-Product Attention]] (the 1/√d_k scaling for dot-product attention) (Source: [[Attention Is All You Need]]).
- Proposed [[Multi-Head Attention]].
- Proposed the parameter-free position representation ([[Positional Encoding]]).
- Co-author of the Mixture-of-Experts paper (cited as [26] by the Transformer paper).

## Connections

- Affiliated with: [[Google Brain]].
- Co-author with: [[Ashish Vaswani]], [[Niki Parmar]], [[Jakob Uszkoreit]], [[Llion Jones]], [[Aidan N. Gomez]], [[Łukasz Kaiser]], [[Illia Polosukhin]].
- Developed: [[Transformer]], specifically [[Scaled Dot-Product Attention]] and [[Multi-Head Attention]].
